About Us

Company Profile

Sheldon's Print - a black owned company - was founded by Sheldon and Claudia in 1997. The primary objective of the company is to participate in the printing industry by capturing a sizable share of the market.

Values

  • To value commitment and devotion to client needs

  • To value the creation of excellence in all the printing we do

  • To value all our clients and employees as unique contributes to the success of the business

  • To focus on challenges and success, rather than on failures

  • To meet all dead lines set by our clients without inferiority to our service

Our Focus

To penetrate the printing market, whilst ensuring we do not loose focus. In addition, to always provide our clients with superior printing quality in all the jobs we do and grow the business. To have a force and input in your business that will be quality driven.

Management

The company is managed by Sheldon and Claudia. Marketing and Sales is handled by Sheldon, whilst Claudia handles the Accounting department.
